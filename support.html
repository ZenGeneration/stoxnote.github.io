Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: How do I backup my data?
A: StoxNote automatically backs up your data to iCloud. Make sure you are signed in to iCloud on your device.

Q: How do I restore my data?
A: Open StoxNote on a device signed in with the same iCloud account, and your data will sync automatically.
